LinkedIn API Setup

Connect your LinkedIn account for automated posting.

1

Create a LinkedIn Developer App

Go to the LinkedIn Developer Portal. Create a new app, select your ETKM company page, and accept the terms.

2

Request Products & Scopes

In your app's Products tab, request access to Share on LinkedIn and Sign In with LinkedIn using OpenID Connect. Then under OAuth 2.0 settings, request the offline_access scope.

⚠ Without offline_access the token cannot be auto-refreshed and you'll have to re-authorize manually every 60 days.

3

Add Redirect URL
