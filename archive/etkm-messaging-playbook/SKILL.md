## Universal Messaging Rules (Apply to Every Segment)

### The 7 Non-Negotiables
1. **The Student Is Always the Hero** — ETKM is the guide. Never the center. Never the hero.
2. **Internal Problem First** — Speak to the emotional fear, not just the surface problem. Internal drives action.
3. **Empathy Before Authority** — Lead with understanding. Follow with credibility.
4. **Plan + CTA Always** — Never end a message in a problem or a stake. Always move to plan and CTA.
5. **Tone Is Audience-Specific** — Warm for families, direct for men, zero-pressure for survivors. Match the tone chart.
6. **Identity Transformation Is the Product** — Students don't buy classes. They buy who they're becoming.
7. **Prohibited Always:** Aggressive language, graphic violence, victim statistics without resolution, overpromises, judgment for past inaction.
