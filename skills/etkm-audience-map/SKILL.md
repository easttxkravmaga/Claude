# ETKM Master Audience Map

ETKM serves 14 distinct audience segments. Before writing any audience-specific content,
identify the segment and load its reference file for full detail.

## Shared Motivations Across All Segments

Every ETKM audience member shares some version of these core desires:
- A desire for genuine capability — not the illusion of it
- A sense of personal responsibility for their safety and/or their family's
- Frustration with training options that feel performative, impractical, or incomplete
- A desire to feel confident moving through the world

## How to Identify the Right Segment

Ask or infer from context:
1. **Who is this content for?** (age, gender, profession, life situation)
2. **What's their primary motivation?** (family, career, healing, fitness, professional role)
3. **What's their likely barrier to starting?** (cost, fear, time, past experience)
4. **What tone do they need?** (See tone reference in messaging playbook skill)

Once identified, load the relevant reference file and use the full segment profile
to shape voice, hooks, messaging angles, and CTAs.
