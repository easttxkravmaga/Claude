# Brand Identity

## Who We Are

East Texas Krav Maga (ETKM) is the self-defense training center for everyday East Texans who want to protect themselves and the people they love. We are not a sport fighting gym, a traditional martial arts school, or a fitness studio. We are a practical self-protection program built for real-world threats.

## Mission

To help everyday East Texans gain the confidence and skills to protect themselves and their families from real-world threats — empowering them to live with strength, freedom, and peace of mind.

## Core Values

**Practicality over prestige.** ETKM teaches what works — not what looks impressive in a tournament or earns a colored belt. Every technique has a purpose: get home safe.

**Accessibility.** Self-defense is not just for athletes, law enforcement, or people with martial arts backgrounds. ETKM is built for ordinary people at every fitness level, age, and experience level.

**Empowerment.** The goal is to give students tools, build genuine confidence, and help them become the capable protectors their families need.

**Community.** ETKM students train together, support each other, and share a commitment to being ready. The gym is a safe, supportive environment — not a place for ego or intimidation.

## Differentiators

**vs. Traditional Martial Arts:**
Traditional arts emphasize forms, rank, and sport competition. ETKM emphasizes threat recognition, de-escalation, and efficient physical response. No belts, no katas, no tournaments — just skills that work.

**vs. "Tough Guy" Self-Defense Programs:**
Many self-defense courses use fear, aggression, and military imagery to market themselves. ETKM uses empathy. Our students include parents, teachers, healthcare workers, and seniors. We never shame people for their fear — we help them move past it.

**vs. Sport Fighting (MMA, Boxing, BJJ):**
Sport fighting has rules, weight classes, and a referee. Real attacks do not. ETKM specifically trains for the chaos, surprise, and psychological shock of a real assault — including multiple attackers, weapons, and confined spaces.

## Who We Serve — The ETKM Hero

The ETKM student is an everyday East Texan — a working parent, a college student, a professional, a senior — who has felt unsafe or vulnerable and decided to do something about it. They are not looking for a lifestyle sport. They want practical tools and the confidence to use them.

**Common entry points:**
- A near-miss or local crime report that made them realize they were not prepared
- Concern about a family member's safety (especially college-age children or elderly parents)
- A career in a high-risk environment (healthcare, teaching, late-night work)
- General desire to feel more capable and less afraid

## What ETKM Is Not

- A competition gym or sparring club
- A fitness program (fitness is a byproduct, not the purpose)
- A traditional martial arts school with ranks and ceremonies
- A military or law enforcement training program
