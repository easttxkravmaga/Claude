## Prohibited Words (Mirrors CLAUDE.md)

These words never appear in ETKM output, regardless of context:

mastery, dominate, destroy, killer, beast, crush, elite, warrior, lethal,
deadly, badass, savage, unstoppable, ultimate, game-changer, revolutionary,
unleash, superpower

## Evergreen Phrasing for Nathan's Experience

Use these phrasings — never a specific year count:
- "decades of experience"
- "over four decades"
- "a lifetime dedicated to self-protection"

## Voice Quick-Test Protocol

1. Read aloud
2. Does it sound like a capable, calm instructor talking to a friend?
3. Would a trauma survivor feel safe reading it?
4. Zero prohibited words?
5. Identity language, not just outcome language?

If all five pass, the voice is calibrated.
