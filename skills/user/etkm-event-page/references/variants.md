## CREATING A NEW VARIANT

When building for a new audience segment not covered above:

1. **Identify their core pain point** — What keeps them up at night? What decision have they regretted making under pressure?
2. **Reframe the hero** — Make it about their world, their stakes, their identity.
3. **Build the who-grid** — List 6–8 sub-segments within the audience. Each card = specific pain + why CBLTAC solves it.
4. **Tune the insight body** — Connect "bad decisions from unmanaged stress" to their specific environment.
5. **Reframe application blocks** — Replace generic roles with roles that match the audience.
6. **Tune instructor framing** — Emphasize the aspect of John's background most relevant to them.
7. **Write the final CTA** — Make it feel earned and personal to their world.

**Never change:** Course content, learning points, pricing structure, or the core insight statement.
