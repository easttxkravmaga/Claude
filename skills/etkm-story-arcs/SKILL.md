# ETKM Story Arcs

Story arcs are the narrative engine behind ETKM's most effective content. A story arc
takes a real or representative student journey and maps it through the full transformation
— before, the decision, the training, and who they become.

---

## Universal Story Structure (Apply to All Arcs)

Every ETKM story arc follows this sequence:

1. **Ordinary World** — Who they were before. Their life, their fear, their limitation.
2. **The Problem / Inciting Moment** — What made the status quo unacceptable. The thing that
   made them finally decide.
3. **Discovery of ETKM** — How they found the guide. Their hesitation. What lowered the
   barrier (free trial, recommendation, etc.)
4. **Training Begins** — What early training felt like. The small wins that built belief.
5. **The Transformation** — The moment they felt different. The new capability. The identity shift.
6. **The New World** — Who they are now. How life has changed. What they can do that they
   couldn't before.
7. **The Invitation** — A gentle, direct invitation for the reader to begin their own arc.

## Key Narrative Principles

**Show the internal state, not just the external event.**
Weak: "She didn't know self-defense."
Strong: "She changed her route home every week. Fear had its own logic, and she'd been following it for years."

**The transformation is always identity-level, not just skill-level.**
Weak: "After training, she knew how to throw a punch."
Strong: "After training, she stopped looking over her shoulder. Not because the world had changed — but because she had."

**The invitation at the end is never pushy — it's earned.**
"If any of this sounds familiar — the weight, the wondering, the quiet wish that you were more
prepared — your first step is a free class. No commitment. Just one hour that might change a lot."
